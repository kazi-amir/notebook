# Target Shopping Guide

Target organizes products in a two-level hierarchy: Departments (broad) and Categories (specific).

### Product Sources

Target's catalog of ~3,600 products is sourced from multiple specialty stores, providing a broad selection across all departments.

## Target Circle

Target Circle is the free loyalty program offering:
- **Circle Deals**: ~30% of products have special Circle member pricing
- **1% earnings**: Earn on every purchase to redeem later
- **Birthday reward**: Special discount on your birthday

### Finding Circle Deals
- Use `circle-deals` command to browse current Circle member deals
- Add `--circle-deals-only` flag when searching to filter for deals only
- Compare `price` vs `original_price` to see savings amount

## Fulfillment Options

| Option | Description | Speed |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Delivery** | Ship to your address | 2-5 business days |
| **Pickup** | Order online, pick up at store | Ready in 2 hours |
| **Drive Up** | Order online, stay in your car | Ready in 2 hours |

All products support all three fulfillment options.

## Spending Analysis Tips

### Monthly Trends
- Compare `spending --days 30` vs `spending --days 90` for trend analysis
- Look for seasonal patterns (holiday shopping, back-to-school)

### Department Balance
- Check spending distribution across departments
- Identify if shopping is concentrated in a few departments

### Category Insights
- Drill into specific categories to find spending patterns
- Use brand breakdown to identify brand loyalty

### Order Frequency Patterns
- Weekly shoppers: 4+ orders per month
- Biweekly: 2-3 orders per month
- Monthly: 1 order per month

## Smart Shopping Tips

1. **Check Circle Deals** first for savings on planned purchases
2. **Compare departments** for similar items at different price points
3. **Track spending** monthly to stay within budget
4. **Monitor favorites** to catch price drops on frequently bought items
5. **Use Drive Up** for quick pickups without leaving your car
