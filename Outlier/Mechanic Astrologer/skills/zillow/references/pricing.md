# Zillow Pricing Strategy

## For Sellers

### Comparable Analysis
Research recently sold comparable properties within 0.5 miles:
- Filter by similar bedrooms, bathrooms, sqft, year built
- Note both sale price AND days on market
- Adjust for condition differences (+/- 5-10%)

### Pricing Psychology

| Strategy | Price vs Market | Goal |
|----------|----------------|------|
| Aggressive | 3-5% below | Quick sale, multiple offers |
| Market rate | At comps | Standard timeline |
| Aspirational | 5-10% above | Test the market |
| Make Me Move | 15%+ above | Not committed to selling |

**Key insight:** Better to price right and get multiple offers than overprice and chase the market down. A stale listing (60+ days) signals problems to buyers.

### Overpricing Trap
List 10% high → no showings → price reduction → now looks desperate → sells BELOW what proper pricing would have achieved.

### Market Conditions Matter
- Rising inventory → price sharper
- Seasonal variations affect buyer pool
- Interest rate changes shift affordability bands
- Watch absorption rate (months of inventory)

## For Buyers

### Market Speed Assessment

| Days on Market | Market Type | Strategy |
|----------------|-------------|----------|
| < 14 | Hot / Seller's | Offer quickly, fewer contingencies |
| 14-30 | Balanced | Standard offer with contingencies |
| 30-60 | Cooling | Room to negotiate 3-5% |
| 60+ | Buyer's | Negotiate aggressively, 5-10% below |

### Zestimate Caveats

**Don't anchor to Zestimate — it's an algorithm, not an appraisal.**

- Zestimate typically deviates 5-15% from actual value
- Accuracy varies significantly by location
- Tends to lag in hot markets and overestimate in slower ones
- Doesn't account for property condition, upgrades, or views
- Use actual comparable sales to drive offer decisions

### Offer Strength Factors

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Cash offer | Strongest — no financing contingency |
| Larger down payment | Signals financial strength |
| Fewer contingencies | Faster close (but more risk) |
| Pre-approval letter | Essential baseline |
| Flexible closing date | Appeals to seller's timeline |
| Escalation clause | Shows commitment up to a cap |

### Hidden Costs to Budget

Beyond the purchase price:
- Closing costs: 2-5% of purchase price
- Inspection: $300-500
- Appraisal: $300-600
- Title insurance: 0.5-1%
- Moving costs: $1,000-5,000+
- Immediate repairs/updates: varies
- First year maintenance: budget 1% of home value
